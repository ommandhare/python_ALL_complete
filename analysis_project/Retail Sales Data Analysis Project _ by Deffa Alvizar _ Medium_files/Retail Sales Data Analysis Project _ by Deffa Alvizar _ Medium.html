The project I have to worked to analyze retail product sales data to gain insights that can inform business strategy and decision-making. By examining historical sales data, I have identified key performance indicators (KPIs) that can help optimize the sales process and conduct sales evaluations. The objectives of my analysis are as shown below:
a. Analyze historical sales data to identify group performance.
b. Determine key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure sales effectiveness and efficiency.
c. Identify product sales performance through the group.
d. Develop models to predict future sales and optimize inventory management.
e. Provide actionable insights and recommendations to improve sales performance and revenue growth.

The data cleaning process that I did started from importing data in google spreadsheet then checking the data through filters to ensure there were no typo errors in writing letters and duplicated data. I also identified columns that did not have values and then categorized them according to client validation. For the data analysis phase, I created a pivot table to identify the results required by the project client. In this process I took the key points required by the client. Information such as the total revenue earned from sales, year-to-year revenue for each month and year, and the number of units sold in the period 2018–2022.
